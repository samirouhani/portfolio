Sign Language Translator Browser Extension (Teknofest 2025)

Teknofest 2025 Barrier-Free Living Technologies finalist project. A Chrome extension & backend system that enhances video accessibility by converting spoken audio into real-time sign language avatar animations.

Problem

Traditional captions are not sufficient for deaf users, especially those who are born deaf, as sign language is their primary language and the most effective way of communication.
Existing solutions lack real-time avatar-based translation and broad platform compatibility.

Solution

The solution is a browser extension that automatically translates any video using a 3D animated avatar. The user can just click on the extension pop up and start seeing the translation.

Methodology

Pipeline converts streaming audio → text (Whisper) → sign instruction mapping → avatar animation commands delivered to extension front-end.

My role

Experimented with Three.js, trying to animate a 3D rigged avatar I made with Blender in the extension. Improved my skills in team work. Designed the logo of our team and other graphics.
